About Paws for a Cause

Our Mission

At Paws for a Cause, our mission is to transform the lives of homeless pets in Uitenhage and the greater Eastern Cape by providing safe, nurturing shelters, comprehensive medical care, and compassionate adoption services. We are dedicated to creating a haven where every pet is treated with dignity and love.

Our Vision

We envision a Uitenhage where every pet enjoys a loving, forever home. Through education and responsible adoption practices, we aim to end pet abandonment in our community.

Meet our passionate staff and volunteers who work tirelessly every day to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need.
